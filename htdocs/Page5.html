Albedo Albedo, referred to as the "Kredeprinz" means chalk prince.
Aloy Mathijs de Jonge, the game director of Horizon Zero Dawn, has stated in an interview that while creating the world of the game, they wanted a lead that was strong enough to match the harsh environments and the hostile, intimidating robots. For inspiration, they turned to Sarah Connor for the Terminator movies and Ripley from the Alien franchise.
Amber The way Amber received her Vision was touching. She became an Outrider to find out why her grandfather went missing. However, her mission turned up nothing. Amber's grandfather did not leave anything behind for her. One day, she was reading an ancient book of legends when she came across this phrase: "What you lacked was not wind, but courage. It is courage that has allowed you to become the first flying birds of this world." After realizing Amber should take her destiny into her own hands, her Vision attached to her hip began to glow. She was ready to spread her wings and fly.
Arataki Itto Despite his strong appearance, Arataki Itto actually has an unusual weakness. That is to say, Arataki Itto is allergic to beans (one of the only characters to have a known food allergy). As for the reason behind this oddly specific dietary restriction, there is an interesting cultural note in Japanese mythology. Traditionally, oni are said to be weak against beans, ranging from acting as an unpassable seal to a more severe item. This is the root for soybean-throwing practices during the holiday of Setsubun, in which the beans are thrown indoors in order to ward away oni. More than likely, this is also the reason that Arataki Itto is allergic to beans.

In fact, Barbara is known for importing items with Jueyun peppers as ingredients from Liyue, and many of her fans in the city follow suit. In her dialogue, she will also offer the Traveler one of her spicy energy drinks as it apparently helps pep her up.

This likely is the reason that Beidou took a different path from the rest of Liyue's citizens. For one, her father, Naganohara Ryuunosuke, used to run the business before her until his hearing turned for the worst (source of quite a bit of humor from her story quest).
